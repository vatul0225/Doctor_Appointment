Save Food, Money And Planet


Here at Foody we are dedicated to assist you in cutting down your food waste and expenses by streamlining the way you keep up with expiry dates of your perishables. Use our smart notifications, expired or near-expiry item recipe suggestions and alerts to increase the amount of your donations so nothing is left behind. We aim to help you save the planet in an easy, effective and environmentally friendly way by allowing you to waste less food, be part of creating a greener future while getting maximum Returns on every meal.
